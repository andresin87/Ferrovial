Research & Development
The department has been intensively involved in some R&D projects within the company as the UAV Research Group that is focused on development and implementation of a low-cost, open source small Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) flight research facility.
The goal of this facility is to support high accuracy activities including control, navigation and guidance algorithms, embedded fault detection methods and final compared mapping. Financial founds will be provided by European Union and Andalusian Government.
