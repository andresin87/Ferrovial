This concession, which, with an investment of 580 million euros and a concession period of 65 years, is the most important civil works contract awarded in Spain, includes the design, construction, financing and operation of the R-4 motorway stretch between the M-50 and Ocaña, as well as the M-50 Madrid bypass between the A4 and A2 dual carriageways, the south-east roadway between the M-50 and the M-40, the A2 dual carriageway connection with the East distributor road in San Fernando and improvement work in the M-50 stretch between the A4 and the M- 409. Of all this work, only the R-4 radial motorway between the M-50 and Ocaña, which is 53 km long, is a toll route, which means the rest, another 42 km, is toll-free. The design was performed by the Ferrovial Agroman Design Office, The R-4 motorway starts at the M-50 and links with the A-4 Dual Carriageway at Ocaña. It has 4 junctions and 3 semi-junctions.
