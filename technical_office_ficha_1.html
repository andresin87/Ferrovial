Ferrovial Agroman
Technical Office
The mission of the Technical Office (TO) is to provide engineering expertise and know-how to our company. This kind of in-house engineering service is unique to Ferrovial Agroman within the construction sector and allows our organization to be more competitive and flexible when taking on major infrastructure projects

Since 1954 the TO has provided innovative, cost-effective and constructible designs for our clients, while meeting the highest standards of safety and sustainability.

The key to this success is our experienced workforce of over 80 engineers with expertise in all civil engineering and building design disciplines.
