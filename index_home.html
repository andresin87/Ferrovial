Organisation Departments of the Technical Office
Given that Ferrovial Agroman has such a strong in-house engineering service, whose task is to provide and add technical value to the service it offers to its customers, it is a unique case among large construction companies.
The Technical Office includes the main specialities within civil engineering and residential and non-residential building design, structured into the following Departments.
Roads & Railways
Bridges & Civil Structures
Geotechnical engineering
Environment
Building structures
Mechanical & Electrical Design
Hydraulic & Marine Works Design
Architecture
