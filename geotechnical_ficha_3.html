Geotechnics
Scientific and Technical Organizations
To continue our successful track record, we have remained proactive in the development of high-level training programs for young engineers. These are tailored to suit the specific needs of our organization and come under the umbrella of such scientifically recognized organizations as the Polytechnic University of Madrid and CEDEX (Spanish institution for R&D in Geotechnics).



The Geotechnical Area is a permanent member of the board in the following scientific associations:

AETOS: Spanish branch of ITA-AITES (International Tunnel Association).

SEMR: Spanish branch of ISRM (International society for Rock Mechanics).

ATC: Spanish geotechnical committee of AIPCR (International Road Associations).

CNEGP: Spanish Committee on Large Dams, branch of ICOLD (International Committee on Large Dams)
